Step 1 of becoming a cyborg

Your Goals

Research shows that writing down your goals is the first step to achieving them. ConnectorDB will track your progress towards the goals you write below.

Hint: Try to make your goals something actionable. "Lose Weight" is not something you can get up and do. "Exercise" is.

Hint 2: Remember that ConnectorDB is an experiement. We might be able to help you achieve goals for which there is a clear TODO list, but we don't think our algorithms can help you cure cancer...

Star Rankings

Your devices will gather some data for you, but ConnectorDB relies on your rankings to see what you think of your life.

Each box you fill out will correspond to a 10-star rating that you will be prompted to fill in once a day.
